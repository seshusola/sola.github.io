Title: WIRELESS POWER TRANSFER SYSTEM FOR ELECTRIC VEHICLES USING MAGNETIC RESONANCE

Description: The main objective of our project is to design and to develop wireless power system suitable for vehicle using resonant magnetic coupled wireless power transfer technology to electric vehicle charging system. Its working principle is analyzed with magnetic resonance circuit model. In order to improve the space utilization rate, to enlarge the mutual inductance of two coils, and to decrease the high frequency resistance effect, the planar circular magnetizer coils are to be design Moreover, the working process of the circuit is detailed analyzed with magnetic tracking module adopted to ensure that the circuit always works in the best condition. Finally, a resonant wireless transfer system for vehicle charging technology is to be design.
